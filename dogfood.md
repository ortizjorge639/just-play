Here are the feature ideas from tonight's dogfooding session to make tracking feel intrinsically rewarding:

## The Problem
Jorge is forgetting to track sessions because there's no immediate reward/feedback for doing it. Tracking needs to feel valuable NOW (not just when card evolution ships later).

## Solutions to Build

### 1. Visible Streak Counter (Simplest)
- "3 days tracked 🔥" displayed on home screen
- Immediate gratification for consistency
- No notification needed - always visible motivation
- Loss aversion kicks in (don't want to break the streak)

### 2. XP Bar Visualization (Like Your Pokédex)
- Every tracked session = +XP
- Visual progress bar toward next level/card evolution
- Makes tracking intrinsically rewarding (you SEE the grind)
- Placeholder for future card evolution system

### 3. Session Counter (Quick Win)
- Show raw numbers: "Session #12" or "7 hours logged this week"
- Gives you something to "grind" while building the real feature
- Numbers go up = satisfying

## Why This Matters
- Social accountability worked (posting screenshots)
- BUT tracking also helps YOU (personal progression/leveling)
- Both can coexist: social proof + personal XP grinding
